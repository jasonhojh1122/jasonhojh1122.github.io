Pentecost

Wikipedia

Summary

The descent of the Holy Spirit upon the apostles fifty days after Easter, enabling them to speak in tongues and marking the birth of the Church.

Biblical Reference

Book(s): Acts of the Apostles

Chapters/Verses: Acts 2:1-31

The Pentecost Self-researched

Oil on canvas c. 1545

A large painting in the nave depicting the descent of the Holy Spirit upon the apostles. One of several Titian works transferred to the Salute from suppressed churches.

Pentecost Cupola Mosaic Self-researched

Gold and glass mosaic 12th century

The westernmost dome of the nave depicting the descent of the Holy Spirit upon the Apostles at Pentecost. Flames descend from a central dove representing the Holy Spirit, with the Apostles arranged in a radial composition below.
