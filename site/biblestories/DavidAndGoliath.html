David and Goliath

Wikipedia

Summary

The young shepherd David, armed only with a sling and stones, defeats the giant Philistine warrior Goliath in single combat. David's victory over seemingly impossible odds became a symbol of faith triumphing over brute strength and the underdog defeating a powerful enemy.

Biblical Reference

Book(s): 1 Samuel

Chapters/Verses: 1 Samuel 17

Artworks Depicting This Story

David and Goliath

Fresco 1509

One of the corner spandrels of the Sistine Chapel ceiling, depicting Old Testament scenes of salvation. Central interlocked figures create a clear narrative event—David is shown at the moment of striking down the giant Goliath. The dramatic composition emphasizes the physical struggle between the two figures.
