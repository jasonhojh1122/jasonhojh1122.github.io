Dome of Florence Cathedral

Filippo Brunelleschi Architecture (brick) 1420–1436

Revolutionary dome designed by Brunelleschi. Exactly half as wide as it is tall. The dome consists of an inner and outer shell.

Funerary Monument of John Hawkwood

Paolo Uccello Fresco 1436

Monochrome fresco memorial to the English condottiero Sir John Hawkwood. Demonstrates Uccello's fascination with perspective.

Niccolò da Tolentino

Andrea del Castagno Fresco 1456

Equestrian monument fresco of the condottiero Niccolò da Tolentino, companion piece to Uccello's John Hawkwood fresco.

North Sacristy Intarsia

Giuliano da Maiano Intarsia (wood inlay) c. 1463–1465

Intarsia decoration in the North Sacristy of Florence Cathedral. Intarsia is a form of wood inlaying using variously colored pieces of wood to create images.
