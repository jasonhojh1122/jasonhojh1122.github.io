Justice Between the Archangels Michael and Gabriel

Jacobello del Fiore Tempera on panel 1421

A triptych depicting the personification of Justice flanked by the Archangels Michael and Gabriel.

San Giobbe Altarpiece

Giovanni Bellini Oil on panel c. 1487

A sacra conversazione (sacred conversation) altarpiece showing the Madonna and Child with saints.

Legend of Saint Ursula

Vittore Carpaccio Oil on canvas 1490–1495

Series of large wall paintings about the legend of Saint Ursula, a British princess who, along with 11,000 virgin companions, was martyred by the Huns at Cologne. The series shows Carpaccio's command of perspective and precise attention to architectural detail.

Procession of the True Cross in Piazza San Marco

Gentile Bellini Tempera on canvas c. 1496

Painted for the scuole (school) of Venice, a religious group. Shows a procession in Piazza San Marco with remarkable architectural detail.
