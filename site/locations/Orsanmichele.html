Tabernacle of Orsanmichele

Andrea Orcagna Marble 1359

An elaborate marble tabernacle housing the miraculous image of the Madonna.

Four Crowned Martyrs

Nanni di Banco Marble c. 1409–1417

Part of the sculptural program at Orsanmichele. Features four Roman sculptors who were martyred for refusing to make a pagan idol.

St. Mark

Donatello Marble 1411–1413

Commissioned for the Arte dei Linaioli e Rigattiri (linen weavers and peddlers). Cloth and drapery are more natural and realistic than Ghiberti's St. John the Baptist.

St. John the Baptist

Lorenzo Ghiberti Bronze 1412–1416

Part of the sculptural program at Orsanmichele. Ghiberti developed a new approach to sculpting eyes, cutting out the line of the cornea and dot of the pupil.
