Coppo di Marcovaldo

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1225, Florence, Republic of Florence Died: c. 1276, Siena

Florentine painter in the Italo-Byzantine style, active in the mid-thirteenth century. His fusion of Italian and Byzantine styles had great influence on generations of Italian artists.

Artworks

Baptistery Ceiling Mosaic

Mosaic c. 1270–1300

Part of the ceiling of the Baptistery of Florence, representing the Italo-Byzantine style of the Duecento.
