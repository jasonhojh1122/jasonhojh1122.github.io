Italian Art

Nanni di Banco

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1384, Florence, Republic of Florence Died: February 12, 1421, Florence

Italian Renaissance sculptor, a contemporary of Donatello. One of the artists whose work manifested the transition from Gothic to Renaissance art, finding inspiration in classical Roman sculpture and bringing new naturalism to Florentine art.

Artworks

Four Crowned Martyrs

Marble c. 1409–1417

Part of the sculptural program at Orsanmichele. Features four Roman sculptors who were martyred for refusing to make a pagan idol.
