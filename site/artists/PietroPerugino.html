Pietro Perugino

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1446, Città della Pieve, Umbria Died: 1523, Fontignano

Italian Renaissance painter of the Umbrian school who developed some of the qualities that found classic expression in the High Renaissance. Leading painter of the Perugian School. Known for the breadth and distance of his spatial backgrounds. Raphael became his most famous pupil.
