Domenico Veneziano

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1410, Venice, Republic of Venice Died: May 15, 1461, Florence

Italian painter of the early Renaissance, active mostly in Perugia and Tuscany. Known for his use of light and color, he was an important influence on Piero della Francesca.

Artworks

The Madonna and Child with Saints

Tempera on panel c. 1445–1447

Also known as the St. Lucy Altarpiece. A masterpiece of early Renaissance painting.
