Italian Art

Melozzo da Forlì

Wikipedia

Born: 1438, Forlì Died: 1494, Forlì

Italian Renaissance painter and architect. His fresco paintings are notable for the use of foreshortening. He was the most important member of the Forlì painting school.

Artworks

Sixtus IV Appointing Platina as Prefect of the Vatican Library

Fresco (transferred to canvas) 1477

First surviving papal ceremonial portrait of the Renaissance. Notable for Melozzo's characteristic use of foreshortening.
