Born: 1829, Fontanelice Died: 1877, Milan Source: self-research

Italian architect who designed the Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II in Milan, one of the largest and most impressive iron-and-glass covered arcades of the 19th century. The project was the first example of a monumentally scaled iron and steel arcade in Italy, executed in a Renaissance Revival style.
