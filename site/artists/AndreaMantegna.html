Italian Art

Andrea Mantegna

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1431, Isola di Carturo, Republic of Venice Died: 1506, Mantua

Italian Renaissance painter, a student of Roman archaeology, and the son-in-law of Jacopo Bellini. Brother-in-law of Giovanni and Gentile Bellini.

Artworks

The Foreshortened Christ (Lamentation of Christ)

Tempera on canvas c. 1480

Famous for its dramatic use of foreshortening, showing Christ's body from the feet with the wounds in his feet prominently visible.
