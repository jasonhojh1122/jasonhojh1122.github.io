Antonello da Messina Self-researched

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1430, Messina Died: 1479, Messina Source: self-research

Italian painter from Messina active during the Italian Early Renaissance. His visit to Venice in 1474-76 was instrumental in introducing Flemish oil painting techniques to Venetian artists.

Pietà Self-researched

Oil on panel c. 1474-1476

The only work by Antonello da Messina remaining in Venice from his 1474-76 stay in the city. Originally housed in the Doge's Palace. This work was influential in introducing Flemish oil painting techniques to Venice.
