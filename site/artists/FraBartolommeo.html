Fra Bartolommeo

Wikipedia

Born: March 28, 1472, Florence Died: October 6, 1517, Florence

Italian Renaissance painter of religious subjects. Also known as Baccio della Porta, he became a Dominican friar after the execution of Savonarola in 1498 and gave up painting for several years before resuming. His work influenced Raphael and helped shape the High Renaissance style.

Artworks

The Vision of Saint Bernard

Oil on panel 1504-1507

Depicts the mystical vision of Saint Bernard of Clairvaux, in which the Virgin Mary appeared to him. This work influenced Raphael and shows Fra Bartolommeo's mature style with soft modeling and harmonious composition.
