Masolino

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1383, Panicale, Papal States Died: c. 1447, Florence

Tommaso di Cristoforo Fini, known as Masolino da Panicale. Italian painter best known for his collaborations with Masaccio, including the Madonna with Child and St. Anne (1424) and the frescoes in the Brancacci Chapel (1424–1428).
