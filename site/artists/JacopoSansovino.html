Jacopo Sansovino Self-researched

Wikipedia

Born: 1486, Florence Died: 1570, Venice Source: self-research

Italian Renaissance sculptor and architect, best known for his works around the Piazza San Marco in Venice. Andrea Palladio considered his Biblioteca Marciana the best building erected since antiquity. His Mars and Neptune statues guard the Giants' Staircase at the Doge's Palace.

Mars and Neptune Self-researched

Marble sculpture 1567

Two colossal statues guarding the Giants' Staircase (Scala dei Giganti). Mars represents Venice's power by land, Neptune her power by sea. The statues flank the staircase where doges were crowned.
