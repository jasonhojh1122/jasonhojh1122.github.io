Italian Art

Duccio di Buoninsegna

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1255, Siena, Republic of Siena Died: c. 1319, Siena

Italian painter active in Siena. Considered one of the greatest Italian painters of the Middle Ages, he is credited with creating the painting styles of Trecento Gothic and the Sienese school. Major figure of Sienese Art of the Early Trecento.
