Giovannino dei Grassi

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1350, Milan Died: 1398, Milan

Italian architect, sculptor, painter, and illuminator. He worked on the Milan Cathedral and is known for his illuminated manuscripts, including the Hours of Gian Galeazzo Visconti.
