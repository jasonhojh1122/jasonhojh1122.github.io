Jacopo Bassano Self-researched

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1510, Bassano del Grappa Died: 1592, Bassano del Grappa Source: self-research

Italian painter who took his surname from his birthplace near Venice. Trained by his father, he painted mostly religious paintings, landscapes, and genre scenes. Known for treating biblical themes as rural genre scenes, representing peasants, animals, and the agrarian landscape with great acuity.

Artworks

Jacob's Return to Canaan Self-researched

Oil on canvas c. 1580

Located in the Sala dell'Anticollegio alongside works by Tintoretto and Veronese. Depicts the biblical patriarch Jacob returning to Canaan with his family and flocks. Bassano's characteristic treatment of pastoral and rural scenes is evident in this work.
