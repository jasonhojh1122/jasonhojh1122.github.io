Filippino Lippi

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1457, Prato Died: 1504, Florence

Italian Renaissance painter, son of Filippo Lippi. Mostly working in Florence during the later years of the Early Renaissance and first few years of the High Renaissance. He also worked in Rome and later in the Milan area and Bologna.
