Leon Battista Alberti

Wikipedia

Born: February 14, 1404, Genoa Died: April 25, 1472, Rome

Italian Renaissance humanist author, artist, architect, poet, priest, linguist, philosopher, and cryptographer. He epitomised the nature of the Renaissance polymath and wrote influential treatises on painting, sculpture, and architecture.
