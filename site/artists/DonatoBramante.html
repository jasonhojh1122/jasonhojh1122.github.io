Donato Bramante

Wikipedia

Born: 1444, Monte Asdrualdo (now Fermignano), Duchy of Urbino Died: April 11, 1514, Rome

Italian architect and painter who introduced Renaissance architecture to Milan and the High Renaissance style to Rome. Started as a painter and appeared as an architect in Milan. His Tempietto in Rome marks the beginning of the High Renaissance in Rome.
