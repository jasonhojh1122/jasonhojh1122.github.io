Jacobello del Fiore

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1370, Venice Died: 1439, Venice

Venetian painter in the late fourteenth century and early fifteenth century. His early work is in the Late Gothic style popularized by Altichiero da Verona and Jacopo Avanzi, while his mature work displays a local Venetian style established by the school of Paolo Veneziano.
