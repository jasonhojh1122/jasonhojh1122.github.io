Desiderio da Settignano

Wikipedia

Born: c. 1430, Settignano, Republic of Florence Died: 1464, Florence

Italian Renaissance sculptor active in north Italy. Known as a stonecutter, he was one of the finest marble sculptors of the early Renaissance.

Artworks

Tomb of Carlo Marsuppini

Marble 1453–1455

Monument tomb created by the stonecutter Desiderio da Settignano for the humanist scholar and Chancellor of Florence Carlo Marsuppini.
