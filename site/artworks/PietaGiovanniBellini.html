Pietà

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Giovanni Bellini
  • Location: Pinacoteca di Brera, Milan
  • Medium: Tempera on panel
  • Date: c. 1465–1470

Biblical Context

Lamentation - The Virgin Mary mourning over the body of Christ after the Crucifixion. While not directly described in scripture, it is derived from the burial accounts and represents the sorrow of Mary.

An intimate depiction of the dead Christ supported by the Virgin Mary.
