Italian Art

Crucifixion Self-researched

  • Artist: Jacopo Bellini
  • Location: Museo Correr, Venice
  • Medium: Tempera on panel
  • Date: c. 1450

Biblical Context

Crucifixion - The death of Jesus Christ on the cross at Golgotha.

A predella work with traditional composition from one of the founders of the Renaissance style of painting in Venice. Jacopo was the patriarch of the famous Bellini family of painters.
