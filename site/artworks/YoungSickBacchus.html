Young Sick Bacchus Self-researched

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Caravaggio
  • Location: Galleria Borghese, Rome
  • Medium: Oil on canvas
  • Date: c. 1593–1594

An early self-portrait depicting the artist as the god Bacchus with a sickly, greenish complexion. According to his biographer Baglione, it was painted using a mirror, possibly during Caravaggio's recovery from illness.
