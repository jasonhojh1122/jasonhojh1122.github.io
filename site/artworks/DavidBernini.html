David Self-researched

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Gian Lorenzo Bernini
  • Location: Galleria Borghese, Rome
  • Medium: Marble sculpture
  • Date: 1623–1624

Biblical Context

David and Goliath - Young David defeats the giant Philistine warrior Goliath with a sling and stone (1 Samuel 17).

A life-size marble sculpture commissioned by Cardinal Scipione Borghese. Unlike Michelangelo's contemplative David, Bernini captures the biblical hero in dynamic action, mid-throw with his sling.
