Relief of the Virgin Showing Soldiers the Spring Self-researched

  • Location: Trevi Fountain, Rome
  • Medium: Marble relief
  • Date: c. 1758

Positioned above the right niche, this bas-relief illustrates the legend that gave the Aqua Virgo its name. A young maiden (virgo) leads Roman soldiers to a pure spring, which became the source for the aqueduct.
