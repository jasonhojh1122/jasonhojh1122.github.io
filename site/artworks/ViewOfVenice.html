Italian Art

View of Venice Self-researched

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Jacopo de' Barbari
  • Location: Museo Correr, Venice
  • Medium: Woodcut print (original wooden blocks)
  • Date: 1498-1500

A monumental woodcut print showing a bird's-eye view of Venice from the southwest, also known as the de' Barbari Map. The museum houses the six original wooden printing blocks. When printed on six large sheets, the composite image measures approximately 135 cm × 280 cm, making it one of the largest prints of its time.
