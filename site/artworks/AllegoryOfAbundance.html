Allegory of Abundance Self-researched

  • Artist: Filippo della Valle
  • Location: Trevi Fountain, Rome
  • Medium: Marble sculpture
  • Date: c. 1751-1762

One of two allegorical statues flanking the central niche, positioned in the left niche. The female figure holds a cornucopia overflowing with fruits, symbolizing the abundance that water brings to the land.
