Italian Art

Genesis Cupola Mosaics Self-researched

  • Location: Saint Mark's Basilica, Venice
  • Medium: Gold and glass mosaic
  • Date: c. 1215-1235

Biblical Context

Creation of Adam - The mosaics depict the Creation story from Genesis.

Located in the south cupola of the west narthex, these mosaics depict the Creation story from Genesis. The designs were influenced by the Cotton Genesis, a 4th-5th century illuminated manuscript brought from Constantinople. The cycle continues with scenes of Noah, Abraham, Joseph, and Moses in adjacent cupolas.
