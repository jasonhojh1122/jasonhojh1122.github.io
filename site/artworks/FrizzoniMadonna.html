Madonna and Child (Frizzoni Madonna) Self-researched

  • Artist: Giovanni Bellini
  • Location: Museo Correr, Venice
  • Medium: Oil on panel
  • Date: c. 1470-1475

Biblical Context

Madonna and Child - The Virgin Mary with the infant Jesus.

An innovative depiction featuring the Madonna in an unusual purple gown and pink mantle, departing from the traditional blue. Known as the Frizzoni Madonna.
