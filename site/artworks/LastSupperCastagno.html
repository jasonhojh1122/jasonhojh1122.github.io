Italian Art

Last Supper

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Andrea del Castagno
  • Location: Sant'Apollonia, Florence
  • Medium: Fresco
  • Date: 1445–1450

Biblical Context

Last Supper - Jesus's final meal with his twelve disciples before his crucifixion, during which he instituted the Eucharist (Matthew 26:17-30, Mark 14:12-26, Luke 22:7-38, John 13:1-17:26).

Located in the refectory of the former convent of Sant'Apollonia. Judas is depicted sitting separately on the near side of the table.
