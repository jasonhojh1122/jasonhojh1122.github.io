Portrait of Doge Giovanni Mocenigo Self-researched

  • Artist: Gentile Bellini
  • Location: Museo Correr, Venice
  • Medium: Oil on panel
  • Date: c. 1478-1485

A profile portrait of Doge Giovanni Mocenigo demonstrating Gentile Bellini's skill as the official portrait artist for the Doges of Venice. Giovanni Mocenigo served as doge from 1478 to 1485.
