Baptistery Font Self-researched

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Jacopo Sansovino
  • Location: Saint Mark's Basilica, Venice
  • Medium: Bronze
  • Date: 16th century

A bronze baptismal font designed by Jacopo Sansovino, positioned beneath the central dome of the baptistery. The font is surmounted by the mosaic dome depicting the mission of the Apostles. Sansovino served as Proto (chief architect) of St Mark's from 1529.
