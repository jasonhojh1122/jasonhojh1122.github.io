Procession in St. Mark's Square Self-researched

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Gentile Bellini
  • Location: Gallerie dell'Accademia, Venice
  • Medium: Tempera on canvas
  • Date: c. 1496

Part of a cycle depicting miracles of the True Cross, this large canvas provides a remarkable view of Piazza San Marco in the 15th century, showing the old mosaics on the basilica facade and contemporary Venetian life in extraordinary detail.
