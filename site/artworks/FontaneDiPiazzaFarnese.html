Fontane di Piazza Farnese Self-researched

Wikipedia
  • Medium: Granite
  • Date: Original: 3rd century AD; Fountain conversion: 16th century

Two identical decorative fountains in Piazza Farnese, Rome, incorporating large granite tubs originally from the frigidarium of the Baths of Caracalla. The basins were taken from the Baths and placed in the piazza during the 16th century.
