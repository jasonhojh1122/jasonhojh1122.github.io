The Cardinal Virtues

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Raphael
  • Location: Apostolic Palace, Vatican
  • Medium: Fresco
  • Date: 1511

Three of the cardinal virtues—Fortitude, Prudence, and Temperance—are personified as statuesque women seated in a bucolic landscape. The theological virtues—Charity, Hope, and Faith—are depicted by putti.

Shows Michelangelo's stylistic influence but with less tension.
