The Confirmation of the Franciscan Rule by Pope Honorius III

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Domenico Ghirlandaio
  • Location: Basilica di Santa Trinita, Sassetti Chapel, Florence
  • Medium: Fresco
  • Date: 1482–1485

Four portraits on the right side of the composition are Antonio Pucci, Lorenzo the Magnificent, Francesco and Federigo Sassetti.
