The Tempest Self-researched

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Giorgione
  • Location: Gallerie dell'Accademia, Venice
  • Medium: Oil on canvas
  • Date: 1506-1508

A mysterious Renaissance painting originally commissioned by Venetian noble Gabriele Vendramin. Depicts a soldier and a nursing woman in a lush landscape with a city threatened by a storm. Despite considerable scholarly discussion, the meaning remains elusive. Considered by some the first true landscape in Western art.
