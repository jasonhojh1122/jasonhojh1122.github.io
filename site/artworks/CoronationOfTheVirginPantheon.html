Biblical Context

Coronation of the Virgin - Christ crowning Mary as Queen of Heaven, based on Catholic tradition and Revelation 12:1.

A 15th-century fresco attributed to the Tuscan school, located in the second niche of the Pantheon. It depicts the traditional subject of Christ crowning Mary as Queen of Heaven.
