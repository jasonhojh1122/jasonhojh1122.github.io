Wikipedia
  • Artist: Pietro Perugino
  • Location: Palazzo Pitti, Florence
  • Medium: Oil on panel
  • Date: 1495

Biblical Context

Lamentation (Pietà) - The mourning over Christ's body after the Crucifixion, before the Entombment.

A large and emotionally restrained depiction of the lamentation, showing the dead Christ mourned by the Virgin Mary, Saint John the Evangelist, Mary Magdalene, and other figures. Perugino's characteristic grace and soft, luminous coloring are evident, with a serene Umbrian landscape visible in the background. Displayed in the Sala di Apollo.
