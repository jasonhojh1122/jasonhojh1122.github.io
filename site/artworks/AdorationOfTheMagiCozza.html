Adoration of the Magi Self-researched

  • Artist: Francesco Cozza
  • Location: Pantheon, Rome
  • Medium: Oil painting
  • Date: 1661

Biblical Context

Adoration of the Magi - The three wise men from the East follow a star to Bethlehem to present gifts of gold, frankincense, and myrrh to the infant Jesus (Matthew 2:1-12).

Companion piece to the Adoration of the Shepherds in the Chapel of St. Joseph, depicting the three Magi presenting gifts of gold, frankincense, and myrrh to the infant Jesus.
