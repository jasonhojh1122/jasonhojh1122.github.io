St. George and the Dragon

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Donatello
  • Location: Bargello, Florence
  • Medium: Marble relief
  • Date: 1416–1417

Predella relief originally below the St. George statue. Features a new approach that focuses on light and shadows, letting the figure blend into the background.
