Italian Art

The Baptism of Christ

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Andrea del Verrocchio
  • Location: Uffizi Gallery, Florence
  • Medium: Oil and tempera on panel
  • Date: c. 1472–1475

Biblical Context

Baptism of Christ - The moment when John the Baptist baptizes Jesus in the Jordan River. As described in Matthew 3:13-17, the heavens opened and the Spirit of God descended like a dove, while a voice from heaven declared "This is my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased."

Painted primarily by Verrocchio, but the leftmost angel, the water and rocks behind the angel are attributed to his young apprentice Leonardo da Vinci.
