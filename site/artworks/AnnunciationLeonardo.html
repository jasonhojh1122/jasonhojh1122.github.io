Italian Art

Annunciation

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Leonardo da Vinci
  • Location: Uffizi Gallery, Florence
  • Medium: Oil and tempera on panel
  • Date: c. 1472–1476

Biblical Context

Annunciation - The announcement by the angel Gabriel to the Virgin Mary that she would conceive and bear a son through the Holy Spirit (Luke 1:26-38).

An early work by Leonardo showing the angel Gabriel appearing to the Virgin Mary. The painting demonstrates Leonardo's early mastery of perspective and atmospheric effects.
