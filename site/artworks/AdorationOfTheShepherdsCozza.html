Adoration of the Shepherds Self-researched

  • Artist: Francesco Cozza
  • Location: Pantheon, Rome
  • Medium: Oil painting
  • Date: 1661

Biblical Context

Adoration of the Shepherds - The shepherds near Bethlehem visit the newborn Jesus after being summoned by an angel (Luke 2:8-20).

One of two paintings by Francesco Cozza in the Chapel of St. Joseph, depicting the shepherds coming to worship the newborn Christ child in Bethlehem.
