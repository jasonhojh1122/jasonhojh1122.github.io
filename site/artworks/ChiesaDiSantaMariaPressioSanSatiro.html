Chiesa di Santa Maria presso San Satiro

Wikipedia
  • Medium: Architecture
  • Date: 1482-1486

A carefully calculated illusion choir. Due to space constraints behind the altar, Bramante created a trompe-l'oeil apse that appears to have depth but is actually only about 97 cm deep. This is one of the earliest and most famous examples of perspective illusion in architecture.
