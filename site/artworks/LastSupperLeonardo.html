The Last Supper

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Leonardo da Vinci
  • Location: Santa Maria delle Grazie, Milan
  • Medium: Tempera on gesso, pitch and mastic
  • Date: c. 1495–1498

Biblical Context

Last Supper - Jesus's final meal with his twelve disciples before his crucifixion, during which he instituted the Eucharist and announced that one of them would betray him.

Leonardo's masterpiece depicting the moment Jesus announces that one of his disciples will betray him.
