Italian Art

Judith and Holofernes

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Donatello
  • Location: Palazzo Vecchio, Florence
  • Medium: Bronze
  • Date: c. 1457–1464

Biblical Context

Judith and Holofernes - Judith, a beautiful Jewish widow, saves her city from the Assyrian general Holofernes by entering his camp, gaining his trust, and then beheading him while he slept drunk. The story represents the triumph of virtue over tyranny and of the weak over the powerful.

Bronze sculpture by Donatello depicting the climactic moment of the biblical narrative.
