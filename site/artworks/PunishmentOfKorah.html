Punishment of the Korah, Dathan and Abiram

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Sandro Botticelli
  • Location: Sistine Chapel, Vatican City
  • Medium: Fresco
  • Date: 1481–1482

Biblical Context

Punishment of Korah - The story from Numbers 16 describes the rebellion of Korah, Dathan, and Abiram against Moses and Aaron. God punished them by having the earth swallow them and fire consume those who offered false incense.

Story narrated from left to right:

  • In the left, the earth opens up to swallow the rebels
  • In the center, figures offering false fire to the Lord were consumed by the fire
  • In the right, Moses seeks refuge from Israelites who stoned him
  • Moses' forehead ray is from biblical text (Exodus 34:29-35)
