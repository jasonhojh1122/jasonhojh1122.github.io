Tempietto

Wikipedia
  • Medium: Architecture
  • Date: 1502

Marks the start of the High Renaissance in Rome. A small circular commemorative tomb (martyrium) built on the site traditionally believed to be the location of Saint Peter's crucifixion. Considered a masterpiece of High Renaissance Italian architecture, it exemplifies the balance and harmony characteristic of the period.
