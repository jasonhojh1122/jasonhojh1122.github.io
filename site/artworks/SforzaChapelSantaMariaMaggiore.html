Sforza Chapel Self-researched

  • Artist: Michelangelo
  • Location: Santa Maria Maggiore, Rome
  • Medium: Architecture with travertine and plaster
  • Date: c. 1564–1573

Biblical Context

Assumption of Mary - The altar painting depicts the Assumption of the Virgin by [Girolamo Siciolante da Sermoneta](../artists/GirolamoSiciolanteDaSermoneta.md).

Designed by Michelangelo near the end of his life and completed posthumously by Tiberio Calcagni and Giacomo Della Porta. The chapel features an innovative elliptical plan with a three-part vault and curving side apses, representing one of Michelangelo's most forward-looking architectural experiments.
