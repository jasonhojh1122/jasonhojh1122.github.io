Italian Art

St. Mark

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Donatello
  • Location: Orsanmichele, Florence
  • Medium: Marble
  • Date: 1411–1413

Commissioned for the Arte dei Linaioli e Rigattiri (linen weavers and peddlers). Cloth and drapery are more natural and realistic than Ghiberti's St. John the Baptist.
