Crucifixion Self-researched

  • Artist: Giovanni Bellini
  • Location: Museo Correr, Venice
  • Medium: Tempera on panel
  • Date: 1453-1455

Biblical Context

Crucifixion - The death of Jesus Christ on the cross at Golgotha.

An early career work showing the influence of Andrea Mantegna, Giovanni's brother-in-law. This Crucifixion demonstrates Bellini's development before he achieved his mature style.
