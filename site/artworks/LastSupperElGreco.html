Last Supper Self-researched

Wikipedia
  • Artist: El Greco
  • Location: Museo Correr, Venice
  • Medium: Panel painting
  • Date: 1568

Biblical Context

Last Supper - The final meal Jesus shared with his apostles before his Crucifixion, during which he instituted the Eucharist.

An early work by El Greco from his Venetian period, before he developed the more elongated and dramatic style for which he became famous. The painting shows the influence of Venetian masters during his formative years.
