Italian Art

Triumph of Venice Self-researched

  • Artist: Palma il Giovane
  • Location: Doge's Palace, Venice
  • Medium: Oil on canvas (ceiling)
  • Date: 1582

A ceiling painting in the Chamber of the Great Council showing Venice crowned by Victory. Part of the decorative program celebrating Venice's glory, executed after the devastating fire of 1577.
