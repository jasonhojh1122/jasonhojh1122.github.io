Italian Art

Adoration of the Magi

  • Artist: Gentile da Fabriano
  • Location: Uffizi Gallery, Florence
  • Medium: Tempera on panel
  • Date: 1423

Biblical Context

Adoration of the Magi - The visit of the three wise men (Magi) from the East who followed a star to Bethlehem to pay homage to the infant Jesus, bringing gifts of gold, frankincense, and myrrh (Matthew 2:1-12).

Masterpiece of the International Gothic style. Gentile da Fabriano was a master of naturalistic landscape and atmospheric effects. The lower left corner shows more natural light.
