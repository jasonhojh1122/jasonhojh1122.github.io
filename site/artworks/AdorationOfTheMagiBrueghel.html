The Adoration of the Magi Self-researched

  • Artist: Pieter Brueghel the Younger
  • Location: Museo Correr, Venice
  • Medium: Oil on panel
  • Date: Late 16th century

Biblical Context

Adoration of the Magi - The visit of the Magi (wise men) to the infant Jesus, bringing gifts of gold, frankincense, and myrrh.

A variation of his father Pieter Bruegel the Elder's work with miniaturist detail. Part of the museum's collection of Flemish works.
