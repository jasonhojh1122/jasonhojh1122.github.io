Treasury of St Mark's Basilica Self-researched

Wikipedia
  • Location: Saint Mark's Basilica, Venice
  • Medium: Metalwork, enamels, reliquaries
  • Date: Various (6th-14th century)

The single best collection of Byzantine metalwork and enamels that survives, many items looted during the Fourth Crusade of 1204. Contains sacred objects including the Votive Crown of Leo VI, chalices, patens, and reliquaries holding fragments purportedly of the True Cross and other sacred relics.
