Italian Art

Gates of Paradise

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Lorenzo Ghiberti
  • Location: Florence Baptistery, Florence
  • Medium: Gilded bronze
  • Date: 1425–1452

The third and most famous set of doors for the Florence Baptistery. Contains the first sensuous female nudes of the Renaissance. Named "Gates of Paradise" by Michelangelo.
