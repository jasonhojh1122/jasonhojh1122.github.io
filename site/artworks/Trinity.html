Italian Art

Trinity

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Masaccio
  • Location: Santa Maria Novella, Florence
  • Medium: Fresco
  • Date: c. 1427

Biblical Context

Holy Trinity - The Christian doctrine of the three persons of God: the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. The fresco depicts Christ crucified, supported by God the Father, with the Holy Spirit (as a dove) between them.

A revolutionary work demonstrating Brunelleschi's principles of linear perspective. One of the first monumental Renaissance paintings to utilize linear perspective. The donors are shown kneeling on either side of the holy scene.
