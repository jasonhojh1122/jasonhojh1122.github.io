Genealogy of Christ, with the Life of Christ and the Virgin Self-researched

  • Artist: Paolo Veneziano
  • Location: Museo Correr, Venice
  • Medium: Tempera on panel
  • Date: Mid-14th century

A complex symbolic work showing Giotto's influence. Paolo Veneziano was the founder of the Venetian School and official painter of the Venetian Republic.
