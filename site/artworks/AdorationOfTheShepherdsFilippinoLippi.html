Italian Art

Adoration of the Shepherds

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Filippino Lippi
  • Location: Uffizi Gallery, Florence
  • Medium: Oil on panel
  • Date: c. 1480

Biblical Context

Adoration of the Shepherds - The visit of the shepherds to the newborn Jesus in Bethlehem, as described in Luke 2:8-20. An angel appeared to shepherds keeping watch over their flocks and announced the birth of the Savior.

Painting by Filippino Lippi, son of Filippo Lippi.
