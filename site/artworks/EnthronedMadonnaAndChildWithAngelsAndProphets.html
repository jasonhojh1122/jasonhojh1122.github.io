Wikipedia
  • Artist: Cimabue

Biblical Context

Madonna and Child - The Virgin Mary with the infant Jesus, representing the Incarnation of God.

Also known as the Santa Trinita Maestà. Cimabue was the last great Italo-Byzantine painter.
