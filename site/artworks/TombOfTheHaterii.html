Tomb of the Haterii Self-researched

Wikipedia
  • Medium: Marble relief
  • Date: c. AD 100-120

An ancient Roman funerary monument discovered in 1848 along the Via Labicana. Its reliefs provide valuable documentation of the Colosseum's original decorated appearance, showing the amphitheater with its numerous statues in place. The reliefs are now in the Vatican Museums.
