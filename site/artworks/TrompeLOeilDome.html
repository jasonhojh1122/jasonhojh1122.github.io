Italian Art

Trompe-l'œil Dome Self-researched

  • Artist: Andrea Pozzo
  • Location: Sant'Ignazio, Rome
  • Medium: Painting on canvas
  • Date: 1685 (original, destroyed; reproduced 1823 by Francesco Manno)

A flat circular canvas painted to create the illusion of a cupola, since the planned dome was never built due to disputes with the Ludovisi donors. Using masterful perspective, Pozzo created the appearance of a coffered dome when viewed from the correct vantage point in the nave. The original was destroyed by fire and faithfully reproduced in 1823.
