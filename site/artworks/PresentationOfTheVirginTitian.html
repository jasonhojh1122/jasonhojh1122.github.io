The Presentation of the Virgin at the Temple Self-researched

Wikipedia
  • Artist: Titian
  • Location: Gallerie dell'Accademia, Venice
  • Medium: Oil on canvas
  • Date: 1534-1538

Biblical Context

Presentation of Mary - According to tradition, Mary was presented at the Temple in Jerusalem at age three.

A huge painting (335 x 775 cm) depicting the three-year-old Virgin Mary entering the Temple of Jerusalem. Commissioned for the Scuola della Carità and still in its original location, now incorporated into the museum.
