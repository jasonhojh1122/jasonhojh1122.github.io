Italian Art

Transfiguration of Christ Self-researched

  • Artist: Giovanni Bellini
  • Location: Museo Correr, Venice
  • Medium: Oil on panel
  • Date: c. 1480

Biblical Context

Transfiguration - Jesus revealed in glory upon a mountain, accompanied by Moses and Elijah.

Likely originally from San Salvatore or San Giobbe church. One of several works in the museum representing Giovanni Bellini's mature period.
