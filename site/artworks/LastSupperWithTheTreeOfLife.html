Italian Art

Last Supper with the Tree of Life

  • Artist: Taddeo Gaddi
  • Location: Santa Croce, Florence
  • Medium: Fresco
  • Date: c. 1355–1360

Biblical Context

Last Supper - Jesus's final meal with his twelve disciples before his crucifixion, during which he instituted the Eucharist (Matthew 26:17-30).

Frescoes in the Refectory of Santa Croce. Notable for placing Judas on "our" side of the table, a convention that continued until Leonardo's Last Supper.
