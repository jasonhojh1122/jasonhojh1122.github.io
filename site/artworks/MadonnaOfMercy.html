Madonna of Mercy between St Francis and St John the Baptist Self-researched

  • Location: Pantheon, Rome
  • Medium: Painting
  • Date: 15th century

Also known as the Madonna of the Railing, this 15th-century painting of the Umbrian school is located in the third chapel. It depicts the Madonna sheltering the faithful beneath her mantle, flanked by Saints Francis and John the Baptist.
